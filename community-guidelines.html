Team Minus Community Guidelines

As our community continues to grow bigger, it is now important to set up guidelines that each member shall follow, including our team members and moderators. These guidelines are to be respected at all times in all places that our community members can interact with each other. This includes but is not limited to: our Discord and Minecraft servers and the comments spaces under posts on our social media like X (Twitter), Instagram, YouTube, Reddit, Bluesky, Mastodon and Facebook.
If you happen to have any question about these guidelines, for example if you need clarification, feel free to contact us at mc.teamminus@gmail.com.


Introduction

Last revision: 02 Nov 2025, 15:41 CET
We assume every community member is aware of these guidelines. They will be informed of any changes made to them by announcements on our social media.
What we define as a "community member" is someone present on our Discord server or someone that commented on any content we posted on social media.
What we define as a "team member" is someone that helped or is actively contributing to one or multiple projects of Team Minus. Most, but not all, of them are present in the Team Minus Discord server and have the @Contributor role. Thus, all team members are community members and are subject to these guidelines as well.


Guidelines

First and foremost, use common sense.

I. Shared content must be appropriate for everyone.

This community is partly composed of minors. Content you share, no matter if it is just messages or if it includes media, must be appropriate to see for children. Basically, NSFW content is prohibited (graphic violence, nudity, profanity, slurs, or other potentially disturbing content), but also anything that isn't suitable for a minor to see or read.

II. Social and political discussions

We understand that members may want to discuss social or political topics. Because our team is a small volunteer project with limited moderation capacity, public debates are allowed only when they remain respectful, evidence‑based, and focused on ideas rather than personal attacks. Please follow these simple rules:

  • Keep tone respectful and avoid insults, name‑calling, or targeting people or groups.
  • Avoid organising or escalating heated debates in general chat. Prefer dedicated channels (if available) or direct messages.
  • If a discussion becomes abusive or repeatedly derails, moderators may remove messages, ask participants to continue privately, or apply standard moderation.
  • If you are unsure whether a topic is appropriate for a public channel, default to moving it to DMs or asking a moderator first.

III. Harassment, hateful speech, and discriminatory content

Team Minus is neutral on political views, but not on human condition. Harassment, threats, or content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against people based on their characteristics (physical traits, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, etc.) is strictly prohibited. This includes slurs, praising violence, dehumanising language, and targeted harassment.

Reasonable, contextual discussion or quoting (for critique, reporting, or academic purposes) is allowed only if the intent is clear and the content is not used to promote or normalise hateful ideas. When in doubt, provide context or move the conversation to DMs. Moderators may remove content and take action when rules are broken.

IV. Respect each other.

We do not want to be restrictive, we don't aim to prevent people from sharing things. We try to be pretty relaxed about this, and will mostly be joking the same way as you, with you. Let's keep things that way by respecting each other, making use of common sense and not crossing the line.


Additional Information

Platforms guidelines

Each service we use has its own Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, or equivalent. Make sure to follow them as well. We will report you to the concerned platform(s) if needed in order for them to take action.

Sanctions

Sanctions are handled case-by-case, taking into account:

  • The severity of the rule break
  • Context and intent
  • Previous incidents

Actions can range from warnings to temporary restrictions to permanent bans. Serious violations may result in sanctions across all Team Minus platforms. The final decision on sanctions remains at the moderators' discretion.

Team Minus Moderation and Contact

Our moderators are volunteers, trying to maintain the community safe for everyone. We currently do not take applications.
Contact the team at mc.teamminus@gmail.com, by pinging or DMing a @Moderator on the Discord server, or DMing us on our socials.
Sanction appeals and ban appeals are done via email at the address listed above.
